How it works

Four steps from page to schedule

Sally stays out of your way until you need it. One button, one click, and the work is done.

1

Browse a product page

Find a fixture, finish, or fitting anywhere on the web — manufacturer site, distributor, spec sheet, or PDF catalog.

2

Click the green button

Hit the always-visible SPEC button (or right-click anywhere). Sally reads the full page — title, description, images, structured data, and linked spec sheets.

3

Review the AI draft

Sally proposes a complete schedule row: manufacturer, model, finish, description, zone, and category — pre-filled and fully editable.

4

Accept and keep browsing

One click adds the approved entry to your project schedule. Sally closes and you're back where you were — the button, nothing more.
