Meet Sally

She turns product pages into architectural schedules — no spreadsheets required.

Click SPEC on any product page (Home Depot, Ferguson, Lightology, etc.) and Sally reads the page, drafts a schedule item, and files it in the right schedule. When the project is ready, share a single link with your contractor or client.

Step 1 of 7

Install the Chrome extension

Sally lives in your browser as a Chrome extension. Open the Web Store listing and click Add to Chrome.

Step 2 of 7

Open a product and click SPEC

Go to a product page on any vendor site. You'll see a SPEC button appear in the corner of the page. Click it to start.

Step 3 of 7

Sign in with Google

The first time you SPEC something, Sally will ask you to sign in. One click and you're done.

Step 4 of 7

Pick (or name) a schedule

If the item doesn't fit any of your existing schedules — or if it's your first item — Sally suggests a new schedule name (e.g. Lighting, Plumbing). Accept the suggestion or type your own.

Step 5 of 7

Review & add

Sally reads the product page and drafts the item — manufacturer, model number, finish, price, lead time, notes. Edit anything that's wrong, then click OK to file it.

Step 6 of 7

Open your project

Click View project in the confirmation panel, or jump straight to your projects page any time. Edit anything inline — cell values, schedule names, even the column layout.

Step 7 of 7

Send a share link

Every project has a built-in share link. Open the project, click More ▾ → Copy share link, then text or email it. Anyone who opens the link sees a read-only view with prices, lead times, and stock.

Ready to try it? Go to your projects
